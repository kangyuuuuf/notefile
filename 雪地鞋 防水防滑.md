##### Lift Ticket Policies

-   Lift tickets are non-refundable and non-transferable.
-   Night skiing is scheduled for Thursday – Saturday and daily during holiday periods for 22/23.
-   Student, Military, Senior, Child and other discounted lift tickets require proof of age and/or identification.
-   One Child 5 & Under skis FREE per ticketed adult (over 18).
-   Chairlift hours can change without notice due to unforeseen weather or condition changes.
-   Prices subject to change.
-   Prime pricing includes Christmas Break to New Year’s, MLK, President’s and all Anchorage School District holidays.
-   Please review our [**refund policy** ](https://www.alyeskaresort.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/refund-policy21.22.pdf)

**Please note: Full Day and Half Day tickets now include night skiing when applicable.
There is no night skiing after March 11, 2023. April lift hours are 11am – 6pm.*
